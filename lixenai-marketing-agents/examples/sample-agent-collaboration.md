# Sample Agent Collaboration

## Goal

Create a 14-day LinkedIn campaign to recruit founding LixenAI Agency Partners.

## Step 1: Marketing Director Agent

Decision:

- Audience: partner-facing
- Priority: organic LinkedIn recruitment
- Paid ads: not approved yet
- Production niche: med spa
- Required assets: campaign brief, LinkedIn posts, DMs, landing page section, demo script, QA review

## Step 2: Campaign Agent

Creates:

- Campaign name: Founding Agency Partner Recruitment Sprint
- Offer angle: sell a finished AI Growth System without coding or fulfillment overhead
- CTA: apply to become a LixenAI Agency Partner
- Measurement: replies, applications, qualified applications, live demo bookings, accepted partners

## Step 3: Copywriting Agent

Creates:

- Partner page hero
- "What you own vs what LixenAI handles" section
- CTA copy
- FAQ answers

## Step 4: Social Media Agent

Creates:

- 14 LinkedIn post prompts
- 4 short-form scripts
- 2 carousel outlines
- 20 hooks

## Step 5: Partner Acquisition Agent

Creates:

- 10 first-touch DMs
- 5 follow-up DMs
- Application questions
- Interview questions
- Referral script

## Step 6: Ads Manager Agent

Recommendation:

- Organic-first test.
- Do not launch cold paid ads until proof, funnel data, and fulfillment readiness are documented.
- Retargeting-only drafts may be prepared for later review.

## Step 7: Sales Enablement Agent

Creates:

- 90-second missed-call recovery demo script
- Partner discovery framework
- Objection responses
- Close checklist
- Handoff checklist

## Step 8: Marketing Director Agent

Final review:

- No guaranteed earnings.
- No passive income.
- No employment framing.
- No claim that all industries are ready.
- Med spa remains the production niche.
- Human review required before publishing.
