# Sales Enablement Workflow

## Purpose

Use this workflow to create sales assets that help Agency Partners present LixenAI clearly, ethically, and consistently.

## Owner

Sales Enablement Agent, reviewed by Marketing Director Agent.

## Inputs

- Offer
- Target client type
- Demo flow
- Partner skill level
- Common objections
- Proof available
- Required CTA

## Step 1: Select Demo Flow

Choose one:

- Missed-call recovery
- AI conversation and booking
- Speed-to-lead
- Appointment reminders
- Database reactivation
- Voice AI with human escalation

## Step 2: Build Discovery Script

Discovery must uncover:

- Current inbound lead process
- Missed-call handling
- Booking workflow
- Follow-up speed
- No-show process
- Reactivation process
- Decision maker and buying process
- Current business impact

## Step 3: Build Demo Script

Demo must show:

- Business problem
- Workflow trigger
- AI or automation action
- Human escalation when needed
- Practical outcome
- Next step

## Step 4: Build Objection Library

Include responses for:

- "We already have a front desk."
- "Will this replace my team?"
- "Is this HIPAA compliant?"
- "How much does it cost?"
- "What results can you guarantee?"
- "Can I think about it?"
- "Will this work for my niche?"

## Step 5: Build Proposal And Handoff Assets

Include:

- Proposal section
- Close checklist
- Partner notes
- Client handoff checklist
- Fulfillment handoff reminder

## Step 6: QA

Confirm:

- No guaranteed appointments or revenue.
- No unsupported healthcare compliance claims.
- No claim that every niche is ready.
- Clear partner/client handoff.
- Human review required before use.

## Output Package

```text
Discovery script:

Demo script:

Objection library:

Proposal section:

Close checklist:

Client handoff checklist:

Training notes:
```
