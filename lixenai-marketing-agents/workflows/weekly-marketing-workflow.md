# Weekly Marketing Workflow

## Purpose

Use this workflow every week to set priorities for the LixenAI Marketing Agent Team.

## Owner

Marketing Director Agent

## Inputs

- Weekly business goal
- Launch stage
- Current production niche
- Funnel readiness
- Available proof
- Available assets
- Team capacity
- Priority audience

## Steps

### Step 1: Set Weekly Objective

Marketing Director defines the single most important outcome for the week.

Examples:

- Recruit founding Agency Partners.
- Prepare med spa niche snapshot assets.
- Produce partner sales enablement.
- Validate organic messaging before paid ads.

### Step 2: Confirm Audience And Channel

Classify every planned asset as:

- Partner-facing
- Client-facing
- Internal

Then select the channel:

- LinkedIn
- Instagram
- TikTok
- YouTube Shorts
- Partner application page
- Live demo
- Internal partner training

### Step 3: Select Campaign Priority

Marketing Director assigns Campaign Agent to create or refresh the campaign brief.

### Step 4: Assign Production Tasks

- Campaign Agent: campaign brief and asset map
- Copywriting Agent: core campaign copy
- Social Media Agent: weekly content calendar and posts
- Partner Acquisition Agent: outreach, application, recruitment copy
- Ads Manager Agent: readiness review, organic-first angle testing, retargeting drafts if appropriate
- Sales Enablement Agent: demo script, discovery script, objections, partner training

### Step 5: Run QA

Every asset must pass the QA checklist in `../qa/content-qa-checklist.md`.

### Step 6: Final Review

Marketing Director provides:

1. Go/no-go recommendation
2. Risk notes
3. Publishing order
4. Human review reminder

## Weekly Output

```text
Strategic objective:

Audience focus:

Campaign priority:

Assets to produce:

Agent assignments:

Compliance risks:

Publishing order:

Recommended next action:
```
