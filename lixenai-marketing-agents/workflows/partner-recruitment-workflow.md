# Partner Recruitment Workflow

## Purpose

Use this workflow to recruit qualified LixenAI Agency Partners without making unsupported income claims or attracting passive-income seekers.

## Primary Audience

- High-ticket closers
- Commission B2B reps
- Sales managers
- Agency-curious operators
- AI-curious entrepreneurs with real sales discipline

## Funnel

1. Founder-led content
2. LinkedIn outreach
3. Partner overview
4. Application
5. Qualification review
6. Live demo
7. Interview
8. Partner onboarding

## Step 1: Define Recruitment Sprint

Marketing Director sets:

- Recruitment goal
- Partner profile
- Number of qualified applications needed
- Channels
- Assets available
- Proof available

## Step 2: Campaign Agent Builds Sprint Brief

The campaign must state:

- This is an independent, performance-based partner opportunity.
- Partners create pipeline and close.
- LixenAI handles done-for-you deployment and fulfillment backend.
- Med spa is the current production niche.
- No guaranteed earnings, guaranteed clients, or passive income framing.

## Step 3: Partner Acquisition Agent Writes Funnel Assets

Required assets:

- 10 LinkedIn DM scripts
- 5 recruitment posts
- Partner landing page sections
- Application questions
- Interview questions
- Referral scripts
- Follow-up messages

## Step 4: Copywriting Agent Tightens The Page

Copywriting Agent reviews:

- Headline clarity
- Role clarity
- CTA clarity
- Risky claim removal
- Objection handling

## Step 5: Social Media Agent Builds Organic Calendar

Create:

- 14-day LinkedIn schedule
- 4 short-form scripts
- 2 carousel outlines
- 10 hooks
- 5 founder posts

## Step 6: Sales Enablement Agent Creates Demo Support

Create:

- 90-second missed-call recovery demo script
- Partner discovery call framework
- Objection library
- Close checklist
- Handoff reminder

## Step 7: Marketing Director QA

Final review must confirm:

- Sales ability is the filter.
- No passive income framing.
- No guaranteed earnings or clients.
- Med spa is positioned as production-ready.
- Other niches are conditional or future.
- Human review is required before publishing.

## Example Sprint Prompt

```text
Create a 14-day LinkedIn recruitment sprint for founding LixenAI Agency Partners. Audience: high-ticket closers and commission B2B reps. Offer: independent, performance-based Agency Partner opportunity. Current production niche: med spa. Paid ads are not approved.
```
