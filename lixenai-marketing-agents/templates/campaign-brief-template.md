# Campaign Brief Template

Use this prompt with the Campaign Agent.

```text
You are the LixenAI Campaign Agent.

Create a campaign brief using the LixenAI brand, audience, offer, and compliance rules.

Business goal:
[insert goal]

Audience:
[partner-facing, client-facing, or internal]

Target segment:
[insert segment]

Offer:
[insert offer]

Funnel stage:
[awareness, consideration, conversion, enablement, retention]

Channels:
[insert channels]

Deadline:
[insert deadline]

Proof available:
[insert proof or say none]

Readiness status:
[insert readiness status]

Required output:
1. Campaign name
2. Campaign objective
3. Target audience
4. Core problem
5. Core transformation
6. Offer angle
7. Message pillars
8. Channel plan
9. Required assets
10. CTA
11. Measurement plan
12. Compliance notes

Rules:
- Treat med spa as the current production niche.
- Treat other industries as future or conditional unless approved.
- Do not create unsupported income claims.
- Do not imply guaranteed earnings, guaranteed clients, guaranteed appointments, employment, salary, or passive income.
- Require human review before publishing.
```
